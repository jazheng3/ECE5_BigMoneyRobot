What is the purpose of the motor driver/shield?

  The purpose of the motor driver is to prevent all the power to be given to the motors and not to the other robot parts like the potentiometers. The motor driver allows the arduino to be powered by a 5 volts battery and give out commands to the robot, while enabling a 9 volt battery to power the motors instead.
